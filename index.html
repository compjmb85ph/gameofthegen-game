How to Play

🎯 Objective

Capture the opponent's FLAG, or march your Flag to their back row safely — no enemy piece adjacent!

♟️ Movement

  • One piece, one square per turn.
  • Forward, backward, or sideways only.
  • Diagonal moves are ILLEGAL.
  • Cannot land on your own piece.

⚔️ Battle

  • Move into an enemy square to challenge. Higher rank wins. Equal = both eliminated.
  • SPY beats all officers & the Flag — loses to Private.
  • PRIVATE beats Spy and Flag.
  • FLAG loses to everyone.
  • The arbiter only reveals which color loses — ranks stay secret!

🚩 Winning

  • Capture the enemy Flag.
  • Your Flag challenges the enemy Flag — your Flag wins!
  • March your Flag to the opponent's back row with no adjacent enemy.
  • Opponent resigns.
